cordão de ouro seoul

Capoeira is an Afro-Brazilian art form that incorporates rhythmic music, dance, core-strengthening kicks, and flexibility-improving escapes into playful expression. Capoeira is a game. Friends play-fight in front of a crowd gathered in a ring, called a roda in Portuguese. At Quilombola Recreation we prepare you to play with style and grace, in the manner developed by Mestre Suassuna, the founder of the legendary Cordão de Ouro capoeira group in Brazil.
